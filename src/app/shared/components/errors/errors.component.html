Install MetaMask

You will need a safe place to store your Quantum Dust: MetaMask. Metamask will also act as your login to the game (no extra password needed).

Switch to our network

You need to switch MetaMask to our own Ethereum network. To do this, open MetaMask and add our "Custom RPC" (http://poa.e11.io:8311)

If you have any issues, check this short step-by-step guide.

Unlock MetaMask

It seems that your MetaMask is locked. Please enter your password and click "Unlock".

If you have any issues, check this short step-by-step guide.
